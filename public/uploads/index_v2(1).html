Material Recovery Facilities (MRFs)

We design, build, and operate MRFs for segregation, sorting, grading, and channelisation of dry recyclable waste. MRFs are the critical link between collection and the downstream recycling value chain.

Sorting & Grading
Manual and semi-mechanised sorting lines for paper, plastics, metals, glass, and multi-layer packaging.
Baling & Dispatch
Compaction and baling for efficient transport to downstream recyclers and aggregators.
Recycler Ecosystem
Established relationships with verified recyclers across material categories for consistent offtake.
Material Analytics
Weight-based tracking, recovery rate dashboards, and revenue reporting via Ecosmartly Recover.

Delivery Models

Flexible execution for every mandate

PPP / DBFOT
Design-Build-Finance-Operate-Transfer
Long-term performance-based partnerships where 3R designs, finances, constructs, and operates against contracted KPIs. Revenue and risk shared with the client ULB.
Best for: City-scale integrated SWM, CBG plants, landfills
EPC
Engineering, Procurement & Construction
Fixed-price design and build delivery with defined scope and timeline. Client retains operations after handover, with optional O&M support from 3R.
Best for: Specific infrastructure — MRFs, composting plants, landfills
O&M
Operations & Maintenance
3R takes over existing or newly built infrastructure under a fixed or performance-linked O&M contract — bringing technology, teams, and processes to improve systems.
Best for: Improving underperforming municipal assets
Hybrid
Customised Structures
Bespoke project structures combining EPC for hard infrastructure with PPP for operations, or phase-gated arrangements for phased rollouts.
Best for: Complex multi-component mandates

Applications

Four specialised apps. One unified platform.

🚛
RouteHub
Smart fleet tracking & route optimisation
RouteHub manages the full complexity of urban waste collection — from route design to real-time vehicle tracking to supervisor alerts. Operational across Amritsar, Silvassa, and Delhi.
  • Live GPS tracking of all collection vehicles
  • Dynamic route optimisation based on waste generation data
  • Missed pickup detection and supervisor alert system
  • Trip verification and driver attendance logging
  • Fuel efficiency and fleet performance analytics
  • Citizen complaint and feedback integration
♻️
Recover
Plant & MRF operations platform
Recover is 3R's plant operations platform — used at Bio-CNG plants, composting facilities, and MRFs to track inputs, processing efficiency, outputs, and resource recovery rates in real time.
  • Incoming waste weight and category logging
  • Processing efficiency and throughput dashboards
  • Gas yield analytics for Bio-CNG / CBG plants
  • Material recovery rate tracking for MRFs
  • Compost quality and output volume reporting
  • Maintenance scheduling and downtime logging
💳
Collect
User fee collection & audit system
Collect digitises and audits user fee collection across residential and commercial categories — creating accountable, transparent, and leakage-free revenue streams for municipalities.
  • Household-level user fee collection tracking
  • Collector attendance and route verification
  • Real-time collection dashboards for supervisors
  • Revenue reconciliation and audit trails
  • Exemption and arrears management
  • Integration with Ecosmartly central reporting
🚻
Loop
Smart sanitation & utility pod management
Loop powers 3R's sanitation vertical — managing smart utility pods with IoT integration, usage monitoring, maintenance scheduling, and citizen access systems. See Loop in depth →
  • Smart utility pod monitoring and status dashboards

  • Citizen access and feedback integration
